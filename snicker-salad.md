Snicker Salad
=============

Ingredients
-----------

*   1 sm box instant vanilla pudding
*   1 cup milk
*   8 oz cool whip
*   3 apples, chopped
*   3 bananas, slices
*   3 snickers, cut into pieces

Directions
----------

Mix vanilla pudding powder and milk well. Add cool whip until well blended.
Add apples, bananas, and snickers. Chill in refrigerator.
